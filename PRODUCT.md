# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Sports, Health & Professional Consultants, and Beauty & Wellness Solopreneurs scheduling appointments and managing client reminders on the go.

## Product Purpose
To streamline appointment scheduling, automate client follow-ups/pre-meeting reminders, and manage appointment confirmations directly via WhatsApp.

## Brand Personality
Professional, efficient, reliable.

## Anti-references
SaaS clichés, cluttered multi-step modals, flat grey forms with low contrast.

## Design Principles
- Direct Utility: Focus on speed of action, with clear one-tap triggers (e.g., Send via WhatsApp).
- Client-First Flow: Ensure scheduling and status updates are intuitive, reliable, and accessible on mobile viewports.
- Glanceable Clarity: Clear visual indicators for pending, confirmed, and rescheduled statuses.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High contrast text meeting WCAG 2.2 AA standards; readable typography at small viewport sizes; support for system font scaling and clear visual indicators for notifications/alerts.
